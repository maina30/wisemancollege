About WISEMAN COLLEGE

Founded in 2025, WISEMAN COLLEGE has grown into a beacon of higher education, committed to fostering intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and a lifelong passion for learning. Our diverse community thrives on collaboration, innovation, and a shared pursuit of knowledge.

Our mission is to empower students with the skills, knowledge, and ethical framework necessary to become leaders and innovators in a rapidly changing world. We emphasize a holistic approach to education, blending rigorous academics with rich co-curricular experiences.

Requirements

General admission requirements vary by course:

  • For Computer & Language Courses: Minimum KCSE Mean Grade D (or equivalent).
  • For Other Courses: KCPE Certificate (or equivalent primary school qualification).
  • Additional specific program requirements may apply.
